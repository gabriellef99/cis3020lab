My Favorite Characters

  1. Wayne

    Of course I absolutely adore the star of the show! Wayne is tough guy with a big heart who only wants to protect what he loves most - his sister, his friends, and his dogs!

  2. Shoresy

    Shoresy is not in the show often enough! He is witty and fun character who does nothing but spit out quips, especially towards Riley and Jonesy, his hockey teammates.

  3. Rosie

    Rosie is my favorite of Wayne's Partners. She is kind-hearted and will do anything to help an animal in need. I love a gal who loves animals as much as I do!

  4. Katy

    Katy is Wayne's sister. They are a lot alike in the way that they only want to protect and help those they love. She's a spunky and rebellious spirit with a lot of heart!

  5. Squirrelly Dan

    Dan is just hard not to love with his constant mispronunciations and teddy bear like nature. And that's what I appreciates about him. I would love to gets some "sushis and sashimis" with this guy any day!

Welcome to Letterkenny!

Hello everyone! This is my fan page for the hit Canadian tv show Letterkenny! Letterkenny is a comedy show about a group of Canadians in the small rural town of Letterkenny. It showcases their daily lives as they drink, "get to chorin'", and have a "donnybrook" or two. I enjoy the heavily dry and sarcastic humor of this show. Each character is wonderful, but of course I have my favorite. I am excited to show you more so you can also see why it a great show! Pitter patter, let's get at 'er.

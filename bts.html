Fun Facts & Trivia

  • Letterkenny is a fictional town based on Listowel, Ontario.
  • Wayne and Shoresy are played by the same actor, which is why you never see Shoresy's face.
  • They are played by Jared Keeso who is actually the creator of the show.
  • Jared also made a hockey spinoff called Shoresy!
  • Shoresy is all about the man himself joining a senior hockey team and vowing to never lose again.
  • Jacob Tierney, the face behind Glen, is actually the director, co-writer, and executive producer of the show.
  • Gus, Wayne's dog in the show, is actually Jared Keeso's dog! Who's a good boy?
  • Many of the actors in Letterkenny have appeared in the show Supernatural - another love of mine!

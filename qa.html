Get to Know My Letterkenny Journey

How did you become a fan?

A friend showed me the show! I was hesitant at first because I hadn't heard much about it, but after watching just one episode, I was completely hooked. My friend kept insisting that I'd love the humor, and they were absolutely right. We ended up binge-watching the entire first season together that weekend! Now, I rewatch Letterkenny once a year!

What do you love most about it?

The humor! Letterkenny has this incredibly unique style of comedy that's fast-paced, witty, and full of wordplay. I love how the characters can go from silly banter to heartfelt moments in seconds. The show doesn't rely on laugh tracks or over-the-top situations. It's just genuinely clever writing and fantastic delivery from the cast. Plus, the running gags never get old. Every rewatch, I catch new jokes I missed before!

What would you recommend to someone just getting started?

There are some parts of the show that can be a bit awkward, just push through! It's worth it! The first few episodes might feel a little strange as you get used to the rapid-fire dialogue and unique Canadian slang, but stick with it. By episode 3 or 4, you'll be fully immersed in the world of Letterkenny. I'd also recommend watching with subtitles on for your first viewing. The characters talk fast and you don't want to miss any of the brilliant jokes! And don't skip the cold opens, they're some of the best parts of each episode.

Do you have any favorite fan communities or creators?

Yes! I'm a regular on r/Letterkenny on Reddit. The community there is amazing, always sharing quotes, theories, and memes. Everyone is super friendly and welcoming to new fans. I also follow @letterkenny.meme on Instagram for my daily dose of Letterkenny humor. They post the funniest memes and clips from the show, and their caption game is always on point. Both communities really enhance the viewing experience and make me feel connected to other fans around the world!
